this is a bliki. half blog, half wiki.

most pages start from a real problem: a machine that needed fixing, a setup that was annoying enough to document, or a pile of commands that only make sense once the reason behind them is clear. the point is not to dump configs. it is to keep the useful part: what worked, why it worked, and what tradeoff was being made.

so this site sits somewhere between reference notes and longer writeups:

- short pages keep the sharp edges filed off and explain the non-obvious bits
- longer pages keep the commands, but also the reasoning that makes them reusable later
- opinionated choices stay, but only when the choice actually matters

it is mostly for me, but written so another linux or NixOS nerd can land here and leave with something usable instead of just copying a random snippet blind.
